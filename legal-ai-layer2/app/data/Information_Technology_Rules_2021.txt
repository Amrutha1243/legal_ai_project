This section outlines the primary regulatory mechanisms applied to citizens and
entities operating under the jurisdiction of the Republic of India. Any
deviation from the prescribed norms shall result in immediate notification to
the relevant central authority, initiating an investigation spanning not more
than 90 days. During this period, the accused entity must surrender all relevant
documentation.

No civil court shall have jurisdiction to entertain any suit or proceeding in

this Act to determine. Furthermore, no injunction shall be granted by any court

any power conferred by or under this Act.
